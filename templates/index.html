SecureExam AI

A smart AI-powered solution to ensure exam integrity by detecting cheating behaviors in real-time. Using advanced face tracking and object detection, it alerts when suspicious activity occurs.

Face Absence Detection

Detects when the candidate's face is missing for more than a few seconds.

Multiple People Detection

Identifies presence of more than one person in the camera frame.

Lookaway & Phone Detection

Detects if the candidate frequently looks away from the screen, which may indicate cheating.

Our cheating detection system is an AI-powered proctoring solution that uses computer vision algorithms and real-time video analysis to identify suspicious behavior during online exams. It tracks the candidate’s face, gaze direction, and surroundings to detect anomalies like multiple people in the frame, device usage, or prolonged absence. Detected violations are logged with timestamps and alerts, providing examiners with evidence to ensure exam integrity and discourage malpractice.
